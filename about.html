About

My name is Rob Conner, and I'm about halfway through a midlife career change from being an Oracle e-Business Suite system administrator to a full-stack web developer.

Although I graduated from Galvanize's Web Development Immersive program in February, I've enrolled at Rithm School to expand my skill set and gain additional experience. At Galvanize, I developed solo and group projects with technologies that include HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Node.js, Express.js, RESTful APIs, PostgreSQL, and the WebSockets and Canvas APIs.

I also have eleven years system administration and support experience, which followed my M.S. in Database Development and Administration. Prior to that, I worked in various accounting roles for ten years after completing a Bachelor of Business Adminstration in Accounting.

This is a simple static site (and an exercise in learning Bootstrap and responsive design) that I created as a pre-work assignment for Rithm School. It was initially just a list of episode summaries for the third season of Twin Peaks, but I'm experimenting with additional content such as short actor biographies and...

Thanks to a recent trip to the Seattle area, I was able to take some photos at the site of the Salish Hotel and Spa, a.k.a. "The Great Northern Hotel," and Twede's Diner, a.k.a. "Double R Diner." Adding a page about these filming locations gives me a good excuse to use them, so that will be coming soon (hopefully)!

Finally, apologies in advance for Albert. And remember, the owls are not what they seem.
